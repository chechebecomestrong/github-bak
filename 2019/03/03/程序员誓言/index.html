程序员誓言

发表于 2019-03-03 | | 阅读次数:

鲍勃·马丁大叔（Bob Martin）是软件开发理论的巨头之一。他提出的程序员誓言，是这个行业的基本职业道德。

原文如下：
The Programmer’s Oath
程序员誓言

18 November 2015
在2015年11月18日发布

In order to defend and preserve the honor of the profession of computer programmers,
为了捍卫和维护计算机程序员行业的荣誉

I Promise that, to the best of my ability and judgement:
我保证，尽我的一切可能做到：

  1. I will not produce harmful code.
    我不会写出有害的代码。
  2. The code that I produce will always be my best work. I will not knowingly allow code that is defective either in behavior or structure to accumulate.
    我写出的代码将永远是我最好的作品。我不会故意允许在卫星或者结构上有缺陷的代码。
  3. I will produce, with each release, a quick, sure, and repeatable proof that every element of the code works as it should.
    每次发布时，我都会生成一个快速、可靠、可重复的证据，证明代码的每个元素都可以正常工作。
  4. I will make frequent, small, releases so that I do not impede the progress of others.
    我将经常发布小版本，这样我就不会妨碍其他人的进展。
  5. I will fearlessly and relentlessly improve my creations at every opportunity. I will never degrade them.
    我会抓住每个机会无所畏惧，不懈地改进我的代码。我永远都不会损害它们。
  6. I will do all that I can to keep the productivity of myself, and others, as high as possible. I will do nothing that decreases that productivity.
    我将尽我所能，保持自己和他人的生产力最大化。我不会做任何降低生产率的事情。
  7. I will continuously ensure that others can cover for me, and that I can cover for them.
    我将继续确保支持其他人的工作，并且他们也可以支持我的工作。
  8. I will produce estimates that are honest both in magnitude and precision. I will not make promises without certainty.
    我会诚实地给出数量上和精度上的估计。我不会作出做不到的承诺。
  9. I will never stop learning and improving my craft.
    我将永远不会停止学习和提高自己的技巧
